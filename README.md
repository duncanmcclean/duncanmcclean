### Hi there 👋

I'm Duncan, a Laravel & Statamic developer. I work at [Steadfast Collective](https://steadfastcollective.com) during the day, where I help to craft community-focused web applications.

Outside of work, I spend my spare time working on various Statamic addons:

* [Simple Commerce](https://github.com/doublethreedigital/simple-commerce) - a Simple Ecommerce addon, designed to integrate perfectly with Statamic
* [Runway](https://github.com/doublethreedigital/runway) - Eloquent + Statamic = **Superpowers** (An easy way to edit models in the CP, then view them in the front-end)
* [Guest Entries](https://statamic.com/addons/double-three-digital/guest-entries) - Let your visitors create/update/delete entries from your front-end
* [Duplicator](https://github.com/doublethreedigital/duplicator) - It does what it says on the tin. It lets you duplicate entries, terms & assets.
* [And quite a few more...](https://statamic.com/sellers/double-three-digital)
